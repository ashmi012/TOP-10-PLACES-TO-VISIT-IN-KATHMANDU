more about Shivapuri Magarjun

Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park is a treasure trove of natural and cultural attractions. One of the key highlights is the Shivapuri Peak, offering stunning vistas of the Himalayas and the Kathmandu Valley, making it a favorite among trekkers and nature enthusiasts. The park's lush forests are home to diverse flora and fauna, including rare orchids, medicinal plants, and wildlife such as the elusive clouded leopard and various bird species, making it a paradise for bird watchers and botanists. Cultural attractions within the park include the ancient Baghdwar shrine, where the sacred Bagmati River originates, and the tranquil Nagi Gompa, a Buddhist monastery known for its meditation retreats and spiritual ambiance. Additionally, the park houses the historical Nagarjun Palace, which was once a royal retreat and now offers insights into Nepal's regal past. For adventure seekers, the park provides numerous trails for hiking, mountain biking, and nature walks, each offering unique glimpses into the region's natural beauty and cultural heritage. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat, an adventurous hike, or a spiritual journey, Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park offers a rich and varied experience for all visitors.


How to get there

To reach Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park, you have several convenient options from Kathmandu. The most straightforward way is to hire a taxi or use ride-sharing services like Uber, which can take you directly to one of the park’s main entry points, such as the Budhanilkantha entrance. Public buses and microbuses are also available; you can catch one heading towards Budhanilkantha from the city center, which is a budget-friendly option. Once you reach Budhanilkantha, it’s a short walk to the park entrance. For those who prefer an active approach, renting a bicycle or motorbike allows you to explore the scenic route to the park at your own pace. Whether by taxi, bus, or bike, reaching Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park is relatively easy and offers a perfect gateway to nature right on the edge of Kathmandu.
