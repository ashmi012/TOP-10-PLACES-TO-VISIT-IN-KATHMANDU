Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park?

~Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park is a protected area located on the northern edge of Kathmandu Valley, spanning 159 square kilometers. It is known for its biodiversity, hiking trails, and cultural landmarks.

How can I get to Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park from Kathmandu?

~You can reach the park by taxi, public bus, or by renting a bicycle or motorbike. Taxis and ride-sharing services provide direct access, while public buses heading towards Budhanilkantha offer a budget-friendly option.

When is the Seto Machhendranath Jatra festival celebrated?

The Seto Machhendranath Jatra festival is celebrated annually, usually during the months of March to April, with a chariot procession through Kathmandu.

How can tourists visit Singha Durbar?

~Tourists can visit Singha Durbar by arranging guided tours or visiting during open house events.

What are the main rituals performed at Pashupatinath Temple?

~Rituals include daily offerings, prayers, and aarti ceremonies performed by priests.

What are the nearby attractions to visit along with Pashupatinath Temple?

~Nearby attractions include Guhyeshwari Temple, Aryaghat (cremation ghats), and the Pashupati area's sacred forests.

What are the entry fees and visiting hours for Kathmandu Durbar Square?

~Entry fees vary for foreigners and locals, and visiting hours are generally from morning until early evening, though times can vary during festivals.
