more about Chandragiri cable car

Chandragiri Cable Car, located on the outskirts of Kathmandu, offers visitors an array of attractions and experiences at its summit. The cable car ride itself provides breathtaking views of the Kathmandu Valley and the majestic Himalayan ranges, making it a scenic journey in its own right. At the top of Chandragiri Hill, visitors can explore the Chandragiri Bhaleshwor Temple, a significant religious site revered by locals and pilgrims alike. This temple offers cultural insights into Nepalese spirituality and is a peaceful place for reflection amidst serene surroundings. The hilltop also features Chandragiri Hills Park, ideal for picnics and leisurely strolls amidst lush greenery and fresh mountain air. Nearby restaurants and cafes offer a chance to savor local cuisine while enjoying panoramic views of the valley below. For adventure enthusiasts, Chandragiri offers hiking trails and nature walks that allow visitors to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the area. Depending on the season, additional activities like paragliding or zip-lining may also be available, providing an adrenaline rush against the backdrop of stunning landscapes. Chandragiri Cable Car not only provides access to stunning vistas and cultural heritage but also serves as a popular destination for both relaxation and adventure, offering a memorable escape into Nepal's scenic splendor just a short distance from Kathmandu city.


How to get there

To reach Chandragiri Cable Car, located on the outskirts of Kathmandu, several transportation options cater to visitors: One convenient method is taking a taxi or using ride-sharing services like Uber, available throughout Kathmandu. These services can transport you directly to the base station of Chandragiri Cable Car, ensuring a straightforward journey. Public buses offer another accessible option. Many routes pass through central Kathmandu and extend towards Thankot, near the cable car's base station. Locals and hotel staff can provide guidance on the nearest bus stops and schedules. For those preferring a more private or flexible travel experience, hiring a car or joining a guided tour provides convenience. Many tour operators offer packages that include transportation to Chandragiri Cable Car, allowing you to explore the area comfortably. Upon arriving at the base station, you can purchase tickets for the cable car ride, which ascends Chandragiri Hill. This scenic journey provides panoramic views of Kathmandu Valley and the Himalayan mountains. At the hilltop, visitors can explore Chandragiri Bhaleshwor Temple, relax in Chandragiri Hills Park, and enjoy local cuisine at nearby restaurants, making Chandragiri Cable Car a memorable destination for both relaxation and adventure just a short distance from Kathmandu.
